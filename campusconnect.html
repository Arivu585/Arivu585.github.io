Description

CampusConnect is a robust student management system developed for college environments, aimed at enhancing administrative efficiency and student engagement. It facilitates seamless management of student attendance, multi-choice tests, assignments, and study materials through a user-friendly interface.

Panel

The system operates across three distinct panels:
  • ADMIN PANEL:

    Responsible for adding staff and overseeing system functionalities.

  • STAFF PANEL:

    Divided into roles such as Principal, Head of Department, and Assistant Professor. Each role has specific responsibilities:

    • Principal:

      Monitors all classes' monthly attendance reports and facilitates communication with Heads of Departments.

    • Head of Department:

      Manages department-specific classes, oversees attendance reports, and collaborates with Principal and Assistant Professors.

    • Assistant Professor:

      Add Students, Manages attendance, conducts multi-choice tests, assigns assignments, and uploads study materials for specific classes.

  • STUDENT PANEL:

    Enables students to participate in tests, access study materials, view attendance records, and submit assignments assigned by Assistant Professors.

Key Features

  • Attendance Management:

    Mark daily attendance for students and Generate attendance reports for entire class.

  • Test Management:

    Creation, assignment, and automated scoring of multi-choice tests.

  • Study Material Management:

    Uploading, organizing, and downloading educational resources.

  • Assignment Management:

    Assignment creation, assignment submission by students, and evaluation by faculty members.

  • Communication Tools:

    Messaging features to facilitate communication between staffs like Announcements, updates and events.
